The evident water crisis in the world is detrimental. Here are just a few facts involving the problem.

844 million people live without access to safe water. That means 1 out of every 9 people have no safe water.

Almost 4 million people die annually from water-related diseases.

Without access to clean water, children are forced to take time away from education and a childhood to collect water for their familes.

The average American 5-minute shower uses more water than the average person in a developing country does in a day.
